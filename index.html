Our program

Our journey will last for 8 months where we go through 12 modules, the most challenging the last module where you can collaborate with with one of our partners to build your own project. Our training is in Brussels, Antwerp, and soon in many other places. Our program is most effective when its combined with your self-study, peer learning, and our devoted coaches.

OUR IMPACT

We graduated over 100 people in Belgium and more than 300 globally. In Belgium, over 70% found and retained a high-quality job as a developer.

OUR CORE VALUES

Community

We’re largely dependent on volunteers. That’s what makes us an engaged grassroots community, as our students call it ‘the HYF family’.

Diversity

Our program is adapted to suit people with different backgrounds, often in vulnerable situations. We teach on Sundays to allow everyone to take control of their personal lives during the week.

Commitment

Our program is free of charge, although we ask for strong commitment during our 7 month-journey. And you know what? Many alumni stay actively involved!

Openness

Sharing is caring. Our curriculum is 100% open. We believe in knowledge for the many, not the few.
